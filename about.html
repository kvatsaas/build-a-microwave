Build-A-Microwave

An independent browser game about drafting components from different microwave models and assembling a final build.

Important disclaimer

  • Build-A-Microwave is an independent entertainment project. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by any microwave manufacturer or retailer.
  • All component grades, overall ratings, tiers, simulations, and popcorn outcomes are fictional gameplay values.
  • The game is not product testing, a safety evaluation, a product review, purchasing advice, or a statement about the real-world quality of any appliance.
  • Manufacturer names, product names, trademarks, and product imagery remain the property of their respective owners and are used to identify the products represented as game pieces.

Sharing and privacy

The game does not require an account. A shared build is encoded directly in the link after the # symbol so the recipient’s browser can reconstruct it. The current version does not send the build to a game database.

If advertising, analytics, or additional data collection is added, this page should be updated with a separate privacy policy and the applicable disclosures.
